Do you want Singapore-based assets to be included as a significant part of the portfolio allocated to you?
You may already own Singapore-based assets, including equities, bonds, properties, businesses and cash, or derive a significant part of your savings from income sources within Singapore. You have the option of an investment account that does not include Singapore assets as significant part of its portfolio. Please choose if you wish to include Singapore-based assets in your portfolio.
