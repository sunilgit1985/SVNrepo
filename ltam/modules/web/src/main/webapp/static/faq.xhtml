What is $ymbil?

$ymbil is a dynamic online investment adviser platform that assesses the risk profile of an investor and recommends a risk-appropriate managed portfolio.



Is this a “robo-advisor” like I’ve heard about?

The $ymbil platform is an electronic “robo advisor” program whereby investors can access an online tool that guides them through a quick questionnaire and suggests an investment in one of the 5 risk appropriate portfolios. You receive the benefit of investment professionals who actively manage your Fund once you make the purchase of the account. $ymbil utilizes the trusted investment professionals of Ladenburg Thalmann Asset Management to manage your Fund, not an actual robot.



Who can open an account through $ymbil?

Anyone who is 18 years old or older, holds a permanent U.S. address and is a legal U.S. resident or U.S. citizen.



Is there a minimum amount required to invest?

Through $ymbil, the minimum investment in the Ladenburg Funds is $500 as an initial investment.



What types of accounts does $ymbil support?

$ymbil currently supports Individual, Joint, Traditional IRA and Roth IRA accounts.



Is there an option for an Automatic Investment Plan?

Yes, the automatic investment plan allows you to make automatic monthly or quarterly investments in your account through a withdrawal from the bank account that you provide on the account application form. The minimum for the automatic investment plan is $50.

Your account will be invested in 1 of the 5 Ladenburg mutual funds so you will only see 1 investment in your account. However, through this single mutual fund, you’ll receive a complete asset allocation through the fund’s underlying investments in equity, fixed income and alternative investment exchange traded funds (ETFs).



Will the asset allocations ever change in the fund I’m invested in? What about things like rebalancing? What does that mean? Do you do that?

The asset allocations in your portfolio will periodically change for tactical purposes. The underlying foundation of our investment philosophy is the construction of a globally diversified portfolio based on strategic asset allocation. Strategic asset allocation is a strategy that divides up a portfolio among major asset classes (equities, bonds, cash equivalents, and alternative investment vehicles) in proportions that are consistent with an investor's long-term financial goals and objectives, establishing a "base policy mix". This mix of assets is based on expected rates of return and risk for each asset class. The tactical overlay essentially helps us determine which areas to under or over-weight based on market conditions. We will make a portfolio change where we can either reduce the risk in the portfolio or potentially add a higher rate of return, given a certain level of risk. In general, tactical changes tend to occur 2-6 times per year and are based on current market conditions. Any changes that we make we will rebalance our Funds accounts for the combination of risk and time.
