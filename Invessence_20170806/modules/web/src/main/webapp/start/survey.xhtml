Understanding Your Financial Identity

Financial Identity Assessment

You will be emailed a personalized analysis of your investor profile at the end of the 15 minute survey.

We believe that financial relationships, be it your relationship with your own money or your relationship with a manager of your money, all begins by better understanding who you are and your personality as it relates to financial matters.

Invessence and DyMynd, boutique financial services firms that work with institutions and advisers to improve market engagement, announce they have teamed up to apply research-driven solutions to bridge relationship gaps between digital advisers and today’s tech-savvy investors.

The Financial Identity Assessment is a psychological appraisal that brings to light one’s emotional financial security, financial comfort, values, and key personality factors. Every person who takes this assessment receives a financial identity profile which will provide personalized details aimed at providing a better sense of who they are as they relate to money as a whole.
